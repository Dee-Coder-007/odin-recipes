Pancakes

Description

These fluffy homemade pancakes are a breakfast favorite! Simple to make, they come out golden and soft, perfect with butter, syrup, or fruits.

Perfect for lazy mornings or weekend brunch, these pancakes will be a hit with the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. In a bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form. Flip and cook the other side.
  5. Serve warm with toppings of your choice.
