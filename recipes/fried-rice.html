Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a delicious and versatile dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, eggs, and your choice of protein. It's quick, satisfying, and perfect for using up leftovers.

This version includes scrambled eggs, peas, carrots, and soy sauce for classic flavor. You can customize it with chicken, shrimp, or tofu based on your taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably chilled)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and scramble the eggs. Set aside.
  2. Add more oil, then stir-fry garlic, carrots, and peas.
  3. Add rice and cook until heated through.
  4. Stir in the scrambled eggs and green onions.
  5. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well and serve.
